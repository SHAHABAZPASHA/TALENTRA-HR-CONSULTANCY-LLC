Data Scientist

Toronto, Canada / USA / Saudi Arabia Full Time $5000 - $8000

Job description

Join DataMinds as a Data Scientist and be at the forefront of revolutionizing how organizations extract insights from their data. You will work with cutting-edge technologies including machine learning, artificial intelligence, and big data analytics to solve complex business problems. This role offers opportunities to work with global clients across various industries including technology, healthcare, finance, and e-commerce, driving data-driven decision making at scale.

Responsibility

As a Data Scientist at DataMinds, you will lead data science initiatives from conception to deployment, transforming raw data into actionable business insights.

  • Design and implement machine learning models and algorithms for predictive analytics
  • Analyze large datasets using Python, R, SQL, and advanced statistical techniques
  • Develop data pipelines and automation frameworks for scalable analytics solutions
  • Create compelling data visualizations and dashboards using Tableau, Power BI, or similar tools
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business opportunities and data requirements
  • Deploy machine learning models in production environments using cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Conduct A/B testing and experimental design to measure impact of data-driven initiatives
  • Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders and senior management

Qualifications

We are seeking a highly skilled data science professional with strong technical expertise and business acumen.

  • Master's or PhD in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
  • 5+ years of experience in data science, machine learning, or analytics roles
  • Proficiency in Python, R, SQL, and statistical analysis tools (SciPy, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn)
  • Experience with deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras)
  • Strong knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS SageMaker, Azure ML, Google Cloud AI)
  • Experience with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop, Kafka) and databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
  • Proficiency in data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib, Plotly)
  • Strong communication skills and ability to translate complex findings into business insights
  • Experience with MLOps, model deployment, and production monitoring preferred
