HR Manager / Recruiter

Toronto, Canada Full Time $3000 - $4500

Job description

We are seeking an experienced HR Manager / Recruiter to join PeopleFirst, a leading human resources consultancy in Toronto. You will be responsible for managing comprehensive HR operations, talent acquisition, and employee relations across multiple client organizations. This role offers an excellent opportunity to work in a dynamic environment where you'll impact organizational culture, drive recruitment strategies, and support business growth through effective people management.

Responsibility

As an HR Manager at PeopleFirst, you will lead strategic HR initiatives and ensure excellence in all people-related functions across our client portfolio.

  • Develop and implement comprehensive recruitment strategies to attract top talent
  • Conduct full-cycle recruiting including sourcing, interviewing, and onboarding new employees
  • Manage employee relations, performance management, and conflict resolution
  • Design and deliver training programs for leadership development and skill enhancement
  • Ensure compliance with employment laws and company policies
  • Partner with management to develop strategic workforce planning initiatives
  • Manage compensation and benefits programs and conduct market analysis

Qualifications

We are looking for a strategic HR professional with strong leadership skills and a passion for building exceptional workplace cultures.

  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field
  • 5+ years of progressive HR experience with focus on recruitment and employee relations
  • Professional HR certification (CHRP, CHRL, or equivalent) preferred
  • Strong knowledge of Canadian employment law and HR best practices
  • Experience with HRIS systems and applicant tracking systems (ATS)
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills
  • Proven ability to handle confidential information and maintain discretion
  • Strong analytical skills and data-driven decision making abilities
